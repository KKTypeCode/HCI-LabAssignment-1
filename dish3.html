How to Make Rice

  1. Wash your rice, rinse it at least 3 times or until the water runs clear using a container to hold the rice
  2. Measure your rice and water inside the container by using the knuckle method or a water to rice ratio of 2:1
  3. Cook your rice on high heat and bring the rice water to a boil
  4. After boiling maintain a low heat simmer for the rice to cook
  5. Cook until the water is absorbed by the rice roughly 15-20 minutes
  6. Let the rice sit and covered for 10 minutes
